## Context
Built independent affiliate marketing projects across multiple digital commerce platforms, starting with Accesstrade and currently expanding into Shopee Affiliate Video and TikTok Shop Affiliate channels.

## Problem
Affiliate opportunities offered scalable monetization potential but were highly dependent on external brand campaigns, platform incentives, and fluctuating traffic economics.

## Actions
- Built student referral communities, fanpages, and network-based traffic channels through Accesstrade
- Designed referral operating process for key members with manual proof-submission and tracking workflows
- Planned next-stage paid acquisition experiments to scale traffic generation
- Currently developing early-stage content-driven affiliate channels on Shopee Video and TikTok Shop

## Outcome
- Built practical experience in affiliate acquisition funnels, referral systems, and conversion-based monetization
- Validated effectiveness of community-led distribution in early-stage growth
- Identified structural limitations of platform and brand dependency in affiliate models
- Expanded capability into short-form content commerce ecosystems

## Key Insight
Affiliate models scale most sustainably when operators control traffic sources, diversify platform exposure, and adapt quickly to changing incentives and algorithm dynamics.
