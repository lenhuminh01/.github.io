## Context
Conducted an analytical case study on RevivCart, a simulated e-commerce marketplace platform, to evaluate performance drivers across traffic, conversion, and revenue generation.

---

## Problem
Despite stable traffic inflow, the platform exhibited suboptimal revenue growth, indicating inefficiencies in conversion performance, pricing alignment, and customer purchase behavior.

---

## Actions
- Structured analysis across key commercial drivers: traffic, conversion rate, and average order value
- Identified bottlenecks in the customer journey, particularly at product consideration and purchase stages
- Evaluated pricing and promotion effectiveness relative to perceived customer value
- Assessed demand quality and alignment between user intent and product offering

---

## Outcome
- Identified primary constraints on revenue growth driven by conversion inefficiencies rather than traffic limitations
- Highlighted misalignment between pricing strategy and customer value perception
- Provided structured recommendations for improving conversion performance and monetization efficiency

---

## Key Insight
Revenue growth in e-commerce marketplaces is primarily constrained by conversion effectiveness and value perception alignment, rather than traffic acquisition alone.
